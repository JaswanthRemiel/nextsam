2021 was a tough year for all of us, but it ended peacefully. I've curated a list of my favourite fonts that I used throughout the projects I finished in 2021. I've always believed that fonts are a great way to liven up your projects and give them a fresh, professional, and energetic look.

I hope this provides you with good inspiration for your next design assignment.

## 1. Mont Heavy

![Image Source: Dafont](https://miro.medium.com/v2/resize:fit:1400/format:webp/1*m6ToueEI2V7DF3s-U25DWg.png)

Mont is well-suited to graphic design and any display application. It could easily be used for corporate and editorial design. Mont was designed by **[Fontfabric](https://www.myfonts.com/foundry/Fontfabric/)**.

**Download:** [https://www.dafont.com/mont.font](https://www.dafont.com/mont.font)

## 2. Halimun Script

*Script Font*

![Image Source: Dafont](https://miro.medium.com/v2/resize:fit:1400/format:webp/0*eCfqtiVoXd7ihL_R.png)

**Halimun Script** features a modern and fresh script with a handwritten style that makes it look appealing, natural, fashionable, and appropriate for any project that requires a handwriting aesthetic. It was designed by **Creatype Studio Co**.

**Download:** [https://www.dafont.com/halimun.font](https://www.dafont.com/halimun.font)

## 3. Gilroy

*San-Serif Font*

![Image Source: Dafontfree](https://miro.medium.com/v2/resize:fit:1400/format:webp/0*2jOB0N8IeNJpuQMF)

Gilroy is well-suited to any display application and could easily be used for corporate presentations and editorial design. Gilroy was designed by **Radomir Tinkov**.

**Download:** [https://www.dafontfree.io/gilroy-font-family/](https://www.dafontfree.io/gilroy-font-family/)

## 4. Seventh August Font

*San-Serif Font*

![Image Source: Dafontfree](https://miro.medium.com/v2/resize:fit:1400/format:webp/0*b8thTs_5xSHmc-v1)

**Seventh August** is a beautiful and bold serif font with a unique style and modern look. It's perfect for many purposes such as headlines, sub-headlines, logos, and short body text for magazines, books, and more.

Seventh August was designed by **[Creative Fabrica](https://www.creativefabrica.com/)**.

**Download:** [https://www.dafontfree.io/seventh-august-font/](https://www.dafontfree.io/seventh-august-font/)

## 5. Royal Kevino Font

*Serif Font*

![Image Source: Dafontfree](https://miro.medium.com/v2/resize:fit:1400/format:webp/0*GuEA7UAjcHTjH8VA)

**Royal Kevino** is a modern serif font. With its neat and beautiful arrangement of letters, this typeface looks outstanding in both formal and non-formal designs. Perfect for gorgeous logos and titles, Royal Kevino pairs beautifully with many fonts and works well with any project.

**Download:** [https://www.dafontfree.io/royal-kevino-font/](https://www.dafontfree.io/royal-kevino-font/)
